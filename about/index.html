About us

An ultimate Solution to Weight Management with an Innovative & Safe Method

Nuvitrin is developed by two medical doctors who have specialised in obesity for more than 20 years. After seeing many patients suffering from symptoms of excessive weight-loss and side-effects of diet pills, the two decided to find a new way to help their patients in a healthier & safer way.

Our aim is to help and provide guidance to achieveing successful weight management without side-effects. That's why we only selected and used naturally-derived ingredients like honey, peppermint, olive leaf extract, vitamins and guarana. With Nuvitrin, free yourself form excessive exercising, execessive food reduction and taking diet pills.

We wish you find body balance that will lead to healthier lifestyle full of positive energy.

Now, lighten up with natual feeling of fullness with Nuvitrin!
